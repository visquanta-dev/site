## What Is Dormant CRM Data in Car Dealerships

### What Dormant CRM Data Means

Dormant CRM data is made up of leads that sit with no touch or are left alone in your car shop’s system. These are people who wanted to know more - maybe they used your site, asked for a trade-in, or called your team. But someone at your shop did not talk to them or help them as they should have.

See these leads like dust in your CRM. They stay there for days or weeks if no one is quick to reply or keeps up with new jobs. Maybe their phone or email is old or fake, or your team missed the chance to reach them. So, these leads just sit in your store’s list, never used, left alone.

You can find these old leads by looking at “past leads” in your CRM or in a file. How to tell if a lead is dormant? Look at the last time you talked with them. If no one has sent a note or called in a long time, based on your shop’s rules, that lead is now dormant. Seeing these old leads is key, as they mean lost money you may make.

### What Unused Leads Cost You

Dormant leads are not just old names in your system - they are lost ways to make money. About one out of three leads gets no talk or gets lost, and car shops lose over $100,000 in sales each year from this.

It is much less work and cost to reach out to these leads than to look for new ones. They already want what you sell. It is much cheaper for your shop to talk to them again, than to spend to get new leads.

### Why Good Leads Go Unused

Knowing why leads go dormant is key, so you don’t keep losing money. The main cause is slow replies. People who want to buy cars now want fast notes or calls. If your team takes too long to reply, or if you are not first to answer, leads go away or buy from a shop that moves quick.

Slow or old ways to follow up will make things worse. If your sales staff stays busy or works old hours, leads will slip through and be missed. Many people send a form or email at night, and if you wait till next day, a fast car shop can get that buyer first.

Other things that cause leads to go quiet are bad CRM systems, new workers all the time, not giving leads to the right team, or using the same message for everyone. If you want to get more money from your CRM, you need to fix these problems, use [CRM strategies to revive dormant leads](https://www.visquanta.com/blog/7-crm-strategies-to-revive-dormant-leads), and make use of the money that sits in your system.
